Operators

Spin operators

The spin operators sigma_x, sigma_y and sigma_z are defined such that they obey the spin commutation relations $[Sⱼ, Sₖ] = i ɛⱼₖₗ Sₗ$. Additionally, the ladder operators are defined as $S± = Sˣ ± i Sʸ$. Several combinations are defined that act on two spins.

When imposing symmetries, by convention we choose sigma_z as the diagonal operator, such that for non-trivial symmetry only the combinations that are invariant under this symmetry are implemented. As such, when defining the other single-site operators with a symmetry, an additional virtual space is required to carry the charge, which is by convention chosen as the second space in a (2,1) tensor.
